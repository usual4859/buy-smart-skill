# 🛒 Buy Smart — Claude Skill

A Claude skill that calls out tech marketing lies before you spend money.

---

## The Story

I watched the Mrwhosetheboss + MKBHD video ["How Tech Companies Lie to You"](https://youtu.be/JstGCPsj9wg) and got annoyed.

The video breaks down every trick companies use:

- "Up to" numbers that mean nothing
- Specs that sound impressive but are made up
- Comparing a new product to a 5-year-old one to fake a big improvement
- Peak brightness vs real brightness
- Max digital zoom as a headline camera feature
- "Aerospace aluminum" that also goes into scooters

Then I realized: this is a checklist. A repeatable research framework.

So I turned it into a Claude skill. Now every time I want to buy something, I just share the link or name and the skill runs through every one of those checks automatically and gives me a structured no-BS report.

---

## What It Does

You give it a product — URL, image, or just a name.

It gives you back:

- Every marketing BS claim flagged with the reality
- Real specs that actually matter (not the ones they highlight)
- What real owners say on Reddit after 1 month
- How it compares to the main competitor at the same price
- Price history check
- Who should buy it and who should skip it
- A final BUY / SKIP / WAIT verdict

---

## How to Install

1. Download `buy-smart.skill`
2. Open [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) → Settings → Skills
3. Upload the `.skill` file
4. Done

---

## How to Use

Just talk to Claude naturally:

- "Should I buy the Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra?"
- "Is this worth it?" + paste a product URL
- "Help me pick between X and Y"
- "Thinking of getting the M4 MacBook Pro"

The skill takes over automatically.
